The Gamora Systems logo combines a bold **"G"** letterform with an upward arrow and bar chart — symbolizing growth, data intelligence, and forward momentum. It exists in two variations: a full-color version on dark backgrounds and a monochrome version for light/neutral contexts.

### 🌑 Design Principles

1. **Dark-first** — All primary interfaces and materials lead with the dark metallic blue palette, conveying trust, depth, and technological sophistication.
2. **Cyan as signal** — Electric Cyan is reserved for highlights, CTAs, and interactive elements — always drawing the eye toward action.
3. **Chrome accents** — Metallic grey and polished chrome tones add a premium, corporate feel without visual noise.
4. **Clean geometry** — Hexagonal patterns and sharp lines reinforce structure, data, and precision.
